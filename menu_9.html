The Onam Sadya

A Grand Feast with Paily and Chikku

Welcome to a culinary journey celebrating the spirit of Onam. This elaborate feast is a harmonious symphony of flavors, where tradition meets taste. We are delighted to share this special meal with you.

Thoran

A delicately spiced stir-fry, a celebration of fresh vegetables.

Vegetable Fresh Mildly Sweet

A crunchy and flavorful stir-fry made with finely chopped cabbage, grated coconut, and a touch of green chili. The dish is tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves, offering a simple yet profoundly satisfying flavor.

Avial

The crown jewel of the Sadya, a creamy blend of flavors.

Creamy Tangy Wholesome

A rich and creamy curry made with a medley of fresh vegetables simmered in a thick paste of ground coconut and yogurt. The flavors are mild and tangy, offering a soothing and comforting experience.

Sambar

The aromatic and tangy heart of the feast.

Tangy Spicy Earthy

A deeply flavorful stew made with lentils, a variety of vegetables, and a signature blend of spices. The tanginess from tamarind and the aroma of asafoetida make it a rich and indispensable part of the Sadya.

Beetroot Pachadi

A refreshing palate cleanser with a sweet-sour delight.

Sweet Sour Refreshing

A vibrant and cooling curry where the natural sweetness of beetroot is perfectly balanced by the tanginess of fresh yogurt. It's a light and delicious side dish that adds a splash of color to the plate.

Special Non-Vegetarian Dishes

Fish Fry

Crispy and fiery fried fish, a true delicacy.

Spicy Tangy Crispy

Fresh fish marinated in a bold paste of turmeric, red chili, and vinegar, then fried to a perfect golden crisp. The result is a dish that is tangy, spicy, and utterly delicious.

Prawns Roast

Rich and aromatic prawns roasted to perfection.

Rich Aromatic Savory

Succulent prawns are roasted in a thick, spicy gravy made from onions, tomatoes, and a blend of traditional spices. The addition of coconut milk adds a layer of creamy richness that is simply divine.
